Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon

Description!

If you like seafood, you have to try this incredible and tasty Honey Garlic Glazed Salmon recipe. I used to be a seafood hater. However, I've become a changed man. If you follow this recipe you'll get a delicious salmon on its own, however, when you add the glaze, it becomes so much better!

Ingredients!

  • 4 (6 oz) salmon filets
  • 1/2 tsp kosher salt
  • 1/2 tsp black pepper
  • 1/2 tsp smoked paprika
  • 1/4 tsp blackening seasoning
  • 3 Tbsp butter
  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 6 cloves of minced Garlic
  • 1/2 cup Honey
  • 3 tbsp water
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp sriracha sauce
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice

Instructions!

  1. Pat salmon dry, season with salt/pepeper/paprika and blackening seasoning
  2. Add butter and oil to a skillet over med-high heat, when the butter melts add garlic, water, soy sauce, sriracha, honey and lemon juice for about 30 seconds.
  3. Add the salmon skin down and cook for three minutes, baste it frequently
  4. Broil the salmon for 5-6 minutes and baste afterwards until salmon is carmelized
