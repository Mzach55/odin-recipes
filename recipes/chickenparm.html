Chicekn Parm

Description!

I love Italian food, if you have ever seen me in real life, you will understand why I have put on a few pounds A good portion of it has to do wit this recipe! It is a very delicious and crunchy rendition of a Chicken Parm! One of the best aspects of this dish is that it doesn't get soggy like most chicken parms do, and it's that key factor that I believe makes it taste so delicious!

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast
  • salt and freshly ground black pepper
  • 2 large eggs
  • 1 cup of panko bread crumbs
  • 3/4 grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup olive oil
  • 1/2 cup of tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup of fresh mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese
  • 2 teaspoons of Olive oil

Directions!

  1. Preheat Oven to 450 degrees
  2. Place chicken on plastic, pound firmly and season with salt/pepper
  3. Beat eggs in a bowl and then set aside
  4. mix bread crumbs and 1/2 cup Parmesan cheese in a bowl
  5. Place the flour in a strainer and sprinke it over the chicken on both sides
  6. Dip the coated chicken into the egg mixture and then into the breadcrumbs. Do this for all your chicken
  7. heat 1/2 inch olive oil in a large skillet until its shimmering.
  8. Cook the chicken in the hot oil into golden, generally each side gets two minutes
  9. Transfer the chicken onto a baking dish. Sprinke your sauce, and cheeses equally
  10. Bake the chicken in the oven until all the cheese is melted and the center is no linger pink
  11. Enjoy your delicious meal!
