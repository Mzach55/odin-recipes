Pan-Seared Steak!

Description!

On my journey to becoming a master chef during quarnintine, I discovered this delicious and easy to cook Pan-Seared Steak recipe. I promise you all, this will be one of the best homecooked meals you will ever have. Since it is partially cooked under a tasty amount of Garlic Butter, it will be very flavorful! Also there is a ton of room for adaptaion with this recipe. For example, you could add multiple vegetables like Aspargus, Broccoli etc.

Ingredients

  • 2lbs New York Strip Steaks
  • 1/2 Tbsp vegetable oil
  • 1 1/2 tsp sea salt
  • 1 tsp black pepper
  • 2 Tbsp unsalted butter
  • 2 cloves garlic, peeled and quartered
  • 1 sprig fresh rosemary

Instructions!

  1. Pat dry the steak with a paper towel
  2. Season the steak with the salt and black pepper
  3. Heat a cast iron pan until hot, and then add your olive oil
  4. Drop your steak into the oil and sear them for about 4 minutes on each side, do not forget to sear the sides
  5. Reduce the heat and then add the butter and garloc cloves and the rosemary to the pan
  6. Spoon the sauce ontop of the steak until ready to eat
